About This Knowledge Base

This knowledge base explores the cutting-edge developments in artificial intelligence, particularly focusing on agentic AI communication, protocol development, and the evolution of internet architecture. Our content synthesizes research findings, protocol specifications, and forward-looking analysis to provide insights into the future of AI-driven systems.

Topics include Model Context Protocol (MCP), Agent-to-Agent (A2A) communication, decentralized AI systems, and the intersection of AI with web technologies.
